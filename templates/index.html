About Models

Explore the different models used in our prediction system for accurate analysis.

Decision Trees

Decision Trees are simple yet powerful models that split data into branches to make predictions based on feature values. They are easy to interpret and visualize.

Random Forest

This ensemble method combines multiple decision trees to improve prediction accuracy and reduce overfitting. It handles large datasets well and provides a robust solution.

Gradient Boosting

Gradient Boosting builds models sequentially, each new model correcting errors made by the previous ones. It's known for its high accuracy and is widely used in competitions.

Support Vector Machines

Support Vector Machines (SVM) are supervised learning models used for classification and regression. They find the optimal hyperplane to separate different classes in the data.
